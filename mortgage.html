Understanding Your Mortgage:

  • Monthly Payment: The fixed amount paid each month towards principal and interest. It doesn't include taxes or insurance (escrow).
  • Principal: The portion of your payment that goes towards paying down the original loan amount.
  • Interest: The portion of your payment that represents the cost of borrowing the money.
  • Amortization: The process of paying off debt over time through regular payments. Early in the loan, more goes to interest; later, more goes to principal.

Note: This calculator provides estimates for principal and interest payments only. Actual payments may be higher due to property taxes, homeowners insurance, and potentially Private Mortgage Insurance (PMI).
