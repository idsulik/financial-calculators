Understanding Inflation's Impact:

  • Inflation erodes the purchasing power of money over time. An item costing $100 today will likely cost more in the future.
  • This calculator estimates the future cost based on a constant average annual inflation rate.
  • The chart shows how the cost increases year by year.

Note: Actual inflation rates fluctuate and this calculation provides a simplified estimate.
